Hot Springs

The water from the hot springs comes from the Guaraní Aquifer System, the largest in South America. It constitutes one of the most important underground water resources, on the planet, due to its size and the quality and temperature of the water. In the region, tourists find multiple ways to take advantage of this resource: pools of all sizes, shapes, and water temperatures, relaxing showers, invigorating jet pools, and bubble baths are a temptation to rest, enjoy, and leave stress behind. These hot springs have close contact and communication with nature, surrounded by soft and undulating natural meadows furrowed by the water of rivers and creeks.

Dayman Thermal

Located in Salto on the river that gives rise to its name, it offers varied hotel proposals, spa and private thermal pools, and a water park with games. The municipal area has temperature-regulated pools by each season in a beautiful and well-kept natural environment.

Arapey Thermal

First thermal complex in Uruguay! Located in Salto. You will find open, covered, and semi-covered swimming pools surrounded by gardens and lush green spaces. It offers different options of accommodations: bungalows, motels, and hotels with an All Inclusive system.

Almiron Thermal

Located in Paysandú, they have a particular differential in that they are the only saltwater hot springs in the region with outstanding properties. The municipal area has saltwater and freshwater pools, bungalows, cabins, and camping. There is a wide variety of hotels.

Guaviyu Thermal

Are located in Paysandú on the stream that gives rise to its name. It has open and covered pools seasoned by a wild and exotic environment of Yatay palm trees. Accommodation options include camping, municipal motels, cabins, and private proposals.

Salto Grande Thermal

A place surrounded by nature, especially to enjoy and rest. It is a place ideally designed to share with the family. Full of pools, where adults and children can enjoy hydro tubes, large-scale slides, and lots of fun. There you will find pools with waves, heated pools, waterfalls, and an artificial beach.
