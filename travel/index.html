Uruguay

Uruguay is a South America little and beautiful country with 3.300 million inhabitants that offers attractive natural conditions that allow you to enjoy a wide diversity of types of tourism just a few kilometers away from each other. Traditional sun and beach and urban tourism added to tourism in rural areas and nature, thermal and relaxation, nautical, congresses and events, and social tourism, among others.
