Enjoy the beautiful beaches!

Uruguay has 450 km of coastline along the Río de la Plata and 220 km of maritime coastline on the Atlantic Ocean. It has beautiful beaches with warm water and soft sand. The east coast of Uruguay is a succession of seaside resorts with varied and beautiful landscapes. From the beginning of the Interbalnearia Route in Montevideo to the end of Route 9 in Chuy, on the border with Brazil, Uruguay's beaches alternate between large seaside resorts and rural towns, the calm waters of the Río de La Plata give way to the Atlantic Ocean, greener and more challenging.

Punta del Este

Punta del Este is where luxury and nature converge. It is located in the department of Maldonado, just an hour and a half from the country's capital. Luxurious summer residences, high-rise apartment buildings facing the sea, huge yachts in the harbor, luxury hotels, and restaurants make it the most glamorous seaside resort in America.
